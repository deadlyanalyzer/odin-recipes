Smoked Ribs

Smoked ribs are tender, juicy cuts of meat that have been slow-cooked over a low, smoky fire, absorbing the rich flavors of the wood. Their succulent, fall-off-the-bone texture combined with a caramelized, savory crust makes them a barbecue favorite.

Ingredients

  • Beef Short ribs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Smoked paprika
  • Brown sugar
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Wood chips (hickory or applewood)
