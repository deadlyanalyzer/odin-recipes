Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring layers of wide, flat pasta sheets interspersed with rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and melted cheese. It's baked to perfection, resulting in a golden, bubbly top and a comforting, hearty meal. The combination of textures and flavors makes it a beloved staple in Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cheese Mixture:

  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tablespoon dried)

For the Lasagna Assembly:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (for topping)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (for topping)
